About

This blog is born with the intent to try something new and to keep a record of some personal experiences. It is a personal space that I manage in my spare time. I write about my own interests, mainly concerning computer technology. In short, it is a bit of a nerdy blog.
The idea is to share the little knowledge I have, because maybe it can be useful to other people too.
